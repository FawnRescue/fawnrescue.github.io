About the FawnRescue Mission

Tackling Fawn Mortality During Mowing

FawnRescue aims to reduce the tragic loss of young deer during agricultural mowing. Our open-source drone solution combines thermal imaging, autonomous flight, and AI-powered detection for efficient and reliable fawn rescue.
